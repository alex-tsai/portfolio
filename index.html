Alpha Kappa Psi Fall Rush

Alpha Kappa Psi is the world's largest co-ed professional business fraternity. As a brother of the chapter at Carnegie Mellon University, I designed the branding and promotional material for our fall rush. I needed to communicate both the social and professional aspect of our fraternity accurately.

It was an interesting exercise working across multiple mediums from social media to t-shirts and business cards. This was my first time taking on a project to this scale. I could have done a better job identifying a vision in the beginning, to make each iteration more effective.

Lulu's Boba

I am currently working on creating a new drink's menu for Lulu's Noodles, is an Asian cuisine restaurant in Pittsburgh. My objective was to recreate the drink's menu. I worked with the owners through various iterations from a simple layout to the actual menu with branding and visual hierarchy in mind. Designing the menu was out of my comfort zone. I tend to utilize monochromatic and simpler color schemes in my projects, but this project required a design that was more lively and fun.

Where2

During the spring of 2014, I took part in Pittsburgh's Startup Weekend. The idea behind our product, Where2, was to create an alternative way to find a vacation spot. It flipped the traditional destination first approach to traveling into an interest based approach.

Unlike my standard hackathon experience, I had to consider business value when I designed this. I worked on both the interaction and visual design of the potential product. The limited time taught me about prioritizing in my design process.

POP Analytics

POP, which stands for Prototyping on Paper, is a software product that creates highly interactive prototypes from mockups and sketches. It is created by Woomoo Inc., a startup based in Taipei.

Over the summer of 2014, I researched and curated a list of metrics that were relevant to the product's success. Then, I designed a dashboard that could display the metrics visually. I had to create an overview display of all the metrics at once as well as indepth displays for each metric. Throughout this project, I worked primarily with the founder. I learned to evaluate my designs from the perspectives of businessmen and engineers.
